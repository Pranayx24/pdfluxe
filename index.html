Frequently Asked Questions

Is PDFLuxe really free?

Yes, the core PDF tools on our platform are completely free to use with no hidden fees, watermarks, or forced registrations.

How do you protect my data?

We don't! Because we don't have it. Our application code runs entirely within your local browser sandbox. We literally have no access to the documents you load into PDFLuxe.

Do I need an internet connection?

You only need an internet connection to load the webpage initially. Because it acts as a Progressive Web App (PWA), once loaded, it can process most documents completely offline.
